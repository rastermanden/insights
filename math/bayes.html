Bayes' Theorem Calculator

Positive Predictive Value (PPV):

PPV Formula: Sensitivity × Prevalence / [Sensitivity × Prevalence + (1 - Specificity) × (1 - Prevalence)]

Negative Predictive Value (NPV):

NPV Formula: Specificity × (1 - Prevalence) / [Specificity × (1 - Prevalence) + (1 - Sensitivity) × Prevalence]

Instructions

Enter sensitivity, specificity, and prior disease rate as percentages (0-100).

Example: Sensitivity 90%, Specificity 95%, Prevalence 5%.
